F.A.Q.

Our frequently asked questions.

How can I order a customized doll?

To order a customized doll you must first create an account, and we will provide you with a username and password to access our order form.



Do I need to give a sample if I want to order a customized doll?

It is not necessary to do so since we have available options for you to choose from, but if you want us to follow or copy a specific doll you may upload a reference picture within the order form.



Can I order a doll from your gallery, and still have it customized to my liking?

Yes, you may choose and order from our pre-existing dolls and still have the option to modify them further.



How can I pay for my order?

Upon completing and submitting our order form, payment instructions will be sent to your e-mail.
